Recording

Have your next project engineered by an experienced professional. Anthony Mantz is available to engineer recording projects located in Southern California in the studio or on-location.

2-Track On-Location Recording

With two mics out in front of the group, this is the best option for large acoustic ensembles such as wind ensembles, orchestras, and choirs where there are general no sounds going through a PA system. This service starts at $200 and includes editing and mastering. Spot mics are available on request. Just fill out the form for a quote or to book.

Multi-Track On-Location Recording

This service is for groups that desire more isolation between instruments/voices, and those with a mix of acoustic and electronic sound sources. This service can used to record anywhere from a concert venue to your parent's living room. Fill out the form for a quote.

Engineering Services

If you are working out of a studio that does not include an engineer in the price, Anthony is available to engineer your project in the studio. He is also available to act as a 2nd for large projects that require an extra engineer to keep the project running smoothly. Please fill out the form for a quote.
