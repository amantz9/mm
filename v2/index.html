Mantz Music offers on-location recording and audio engineering services throughout the Southern California region, and now mixing and mastering from anywhere in the world via the internet. I am recording engineer Anthony Mantz, and I have been providing professional recording services since 2004 and I am ready to make your project come to life! Whether you are a student needing a recital recording, a band preparing an independent release, or a label ready to mix a commercial release, check out what I have to offer and let me help you.

Anthony Mantz has been engineering professionally in the Southern California region since 2004. He holds a stong balance between technology and musicality with a B.A. Degree in Music from CSU Fullerton and Pro Tools Operator Certification from The Recording Arts Center at Studio West in San Diego.
